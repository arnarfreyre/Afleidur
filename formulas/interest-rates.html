Forward Rate

$$f(t_1, t_2) = \frac{(1 + r_2)^{t_2}}{(1 + r_1)^{t_1}} - 1$$
Usage: Calculate forward rates from spot rates

Term Structure Interpolation

$$r(t) = r_0 + \text{slope} \times (t - t_0)$$
