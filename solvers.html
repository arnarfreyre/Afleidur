About the Solvers

These interactive solvers are designed to help you understand and apply derivative pricing models. Each solver includes detailed explanations of the underlying mathematics, step-by-step calculations, and the ability to export results for further analysis.

All calculations are performed in your browser using JavaScript for instant results. The formulas and methodologies used are consistent with the course material and industry standards.
