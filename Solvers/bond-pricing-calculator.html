Key Formulas

Bond Price:

$$P = \sum_{t=1}^{n} \frac{C}{(1+r)^{t/m}} + \frac{FV}{(1+r)^{n/m}}$$

Where: C = coupon payment, r = annual yield, t = period number, m = payments per year, n = total periods, FV = face value

Current Yield:

$$CY = \frac{\text{Annual Coupon}}{\text{Current Price}}$$

Macaulay Duration:

$$D_{Mac} = \frac{\sum_{t=1}^{n} \frac{t \cdot CF_t}{(1+y)^t}}{P}$$

Modified Duration:

$$D_{Mod} = \frac{D_{Mac}}{1 + \frac{y}{m}}$$

Effective Duration:

$$D_{Eff} = \frac{PV_{-} - PV_{+}}{2 \times PV_0 \times \Delta r}$$

Convexity:

$$C = \frac{1}{P} \sum_{t=1}^{n} \frac{t(t+1) \cdot CF_t}{(1+y)^{t+2}}$$
