Important Constants

  • 1 basis point = 0.01% = 0.0001
  • Euler's number (e) ≈ 2.71828
  • Days in year = 360 (30/360) or 365 (Actual/365)

Duration Rules of Thumb

  • Zero-coupon bond: Duration = Maturity
  • 10-year coupon bond: Duration ≈ 7-9 years
  • Perpetuity: Duration = $(1+y)/y$ years
