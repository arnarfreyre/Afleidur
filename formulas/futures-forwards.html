Futures & Forwards

Pricing formulas for derivative contracts

Forward Price

$$F_0 = S_0 e^{rT}$$
With Dividends: $F_0 = S_0 e^{(r-q)T}$

Cost of Carry Model

$$F = S e^{(r+s-c)T}$$

Variables:

  • $s$ = Storage costs (as % of spot price)
  • $c$ = Convenience yield

All formulas verified using Python calculations
